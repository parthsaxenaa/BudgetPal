About BudgetPal

BudgetPal was created by a team of former college students who experienced firsthand the challenges of managing finances during college years.

Our mission is to empower students with the tools they need to develop healthy financial habits early, reduce the stress of money management, and focus on what matters most—their education and experiences.

We've designed BudgetPal specifically for the unique financial situations college students face, from irregular income sources to shared expenses and academic term planning.
